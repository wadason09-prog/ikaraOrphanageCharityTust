About Ikara Orphanage Charity Trust

Empowering children and communities through compassion, education and sustainable development.

Our story

Our Story

Ikara Orphanage Charity Trust (IKOCT) is a non-governmental organization established in (2008) in Ikara Local Government Area of Kaduna State, Nigeria. This Trust was established with a mission to cater for the needs of orphans as well as to help alleviate the numerous challenges (especially education) being faced by the orphans in Ikara Local Government Area. The Trust started with five orphans in the first year of its establishment and this number has increased to one thousand three hundred and thirty three (1,408) as at 2025. The Trust is a corporate entity registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) (Registration Number CAC/IT/NO 65707), recognized and duly registered also by the Ikara Local Government Council, and Kaduna State Government. The office of the Trust which doubles as the orphanage is located at No. 1 Kurmin Kogi Close, by Jamfalan Road, Near Imam Malik Ibn Anas Juma`at Mosque, G.R.A Ikara, Ikara LGA, Kaduna State of Nigeria.

Why we exist

Mission & Vision

Mission

:- To improve the lives of orphans in our local community through support in the provision of sound education, moral orientation and empowerment.

Vision

:- To strive for a society were basic needs of every orphan are taken care of through collective effort of the muslim ummah.

Our charter

Objectives Of The Trust

The objectives of the Trust are as follows:- .

01

Basic Necessities Support

To support orphans in the provision of basic necessities of life including food, clothing, healthcare and welfares upport.

02

Moral Upbringing

To partner with guardians in ensuring that registered orphans receive sound moral upbringing, discipline and positive character development.

03

Empowerment Programs

To institute and promote empowerment programmes in partnership with other concerned groups and individuals for the benefit of orphans and their mothers.

04

Quality Education

To ensure that every registered orphan receives quality education including religious education through scholarships and other educational support packages.

What We Do

We provide support to the registered orphans in terms of funding their education, feeding, health care, clothing and empowerment trainings. EDUCATION Currently, we provides educational support to more than Eight hundred and ninety-two orphans (892) at Primary, Secondary and Tertiary levels of education. Feeding

We provide orphans with foodstuff such as grains, vegetable, vegetable oil, sugar, meat, etc.

Healthcare

We settle medical bills of the orphans when the need arises.

Clothing

The Trust provides on annual basis, clothes to more than half of the orphans under its care.

Empowerment

We train orphans and their mothers in different skills acquisition programmes and other empowerment programmes annually for them to be self-reliant.
