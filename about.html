Our story

Our Story

ABOUT US Ikara Orphanage Charity Trust is a non-governmental organization established in (2008) in Ikara Local Government Area of Kaduna State, Nigeria. This Trust was established with a mission to cater for the needs of orphans as well as to help alleviate the numerous challenges (especially education) being faced by the orphans in Ikara Local Government Area. The Trust started with five orphans in the first year of its establishment and this number has increased to one thousand three hundred and thirty three (1333) as at 2025. The Trust is a corporate entity registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) (Registration Number CAC/IT/NO 65707), recognized and duly registered also by the Ikara Local Government Council, and Kaduna State Government. The office of the Trust which doubles as the orphanage is located at No. 1 Kurmin Kogi Close, by Jamfalan Road, Near Imam Malik Ibn Anas Juma`at Mosque, G.R.A Ikara, Ikara LGA, Kaduna State of Nigeria.

Why we exist

Mission & Vision

Mission

:- To improve the lives of orphans in our local community through support in the provision of sound education, moral orientation and empowerment.

Vision

:- To strive for a society were basic needs of every orphan are taken care of through collective effort of the muslim ummah.

Our charter

Objectives Of The Trust

The objectives of the Trust are as follows:-

  1. To support orphans in the provision of some basic necessities of life.
  2. To partner with the guardians in ensuring that the orphans have sound moral upbringing
  3. To institute and promote, in partnership with other concerned groups and individuals, empowerment programmes for the orphans and their mothers.
  4. To ensure that each registered orphan receives quality education, including religious education, through the provision of scholarships and other educational support packages.
.

01

Basic Necessities Support

To support orphans in the provision of basic necessities of life including food, clothing, shelter, healthcare and welfare support.

02

Moral Upbringing

To partner with guardians in ensuring that registered orphans receive sound moral upbringing, discipline and positive character development.

03

Empowerment Programs

To institute and promote empowerment programmes in partnership with other concerned groups and individuals for the benefit of orphans and their mothers.

04

Quality Education

To ensure that every registered orphan receives quality education including religious education through scholarships and other educational support packages.
