Dr. Abdurrahman Sambo

Dr. Abdurrahman Sambo, mni Abdurrahman Sambo was born on February 10, 1956 in Ikara LGA, Kaduna State. He qualified as a Medical Doctor in 1980, having earned an MBBS degree from Ahmadu Bello University (ABU), Zaria. He earned an MHPEd degree under the World Health Organization (WHO) fellowship from University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia in 1992 and also earned a Certificate in Health Planning, Economics and Management as a Chevenning Scholar from Nuffield Institute for Health, University of Leeds UK in 1996. He was admitted into the National Institute for Policy and Strategic Studies, Kuru, Jos, 2003 and became a Member of the National Institute (mni). He began his career as a House Officer and rose to the post of a Registrar of Medicine at the Ahmadu Bello University Teaching Hospital, Zaria between 1980 to 1985. He later served variously in administrative positions at the National Universities Commission (NUC) between 1987 to 2006. Sambo`s wealth of experience has found him in several other administrative positions including Honourable Commissioner for Health, Kaduna State 1997 to 1999, member to several Committees and a pioneer member of the Governing Council of Kaduna State University between 2003 to 2006. He was appointed a General Manager with the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) in April 2006 and was appointed its Acting Executive Secretary between March 2012 and November 2013. He retired from the National Health Insurance Scheme in March 2014. He has since retirement, been engaged as Chairman, YUSAS Farms Ltd, Kaduna and Chairman Board of Trustees of Ikara Orphanage Charity Trust. He is happily married and blessed with 4 children.

Shehu Muhammad Shitu

Alhaji Shehu Muhammad Shitu was born on 15 may, 1952 in Ikara Local Government Area, Kaduna State of Nigeria. He attended Ikara Central Primary School (1958 - 1962) and Soba Senior Primary School (1963 – 1965) for his primary education. He also attended Government Secondary School Alhudahuda College Zaria (1966 - 1970) and Government Collage Kaduna (1971 - 1972) for his secondary school education, he obtained his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration with specialization in marketing from the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University Zaria between in 1976. Alhaji Shehu also obtained Master’s degree in Business Administration in 1999 from the same institution. He began his career with French company, (CFAO) in 1977, where he was posted to Sokoto branch as the pioneer branch manager. In the year 1979, he left CFAO and joined Nigerian Tobacco Company (NTC). He later worked with many companies such as, Songhai Petroleum Company, Farida Investment and Finance Company and Ikara Food Processing Company. In the year 1991, he was at Industrial and Finance Company Ltd. (KIFC) as Assistant Investment Manager. Alh. Shehu was a member of the Board of Directors for several companies which included Kaduna State Housing Authority, Gaskiya Corporation Ltd. Zaria, Polyfibre Industries ltd., Kaduna, Zaria Industries Ltd. He also served as member of Revenue Generation Committee of the Kaduna State University and Council of Kaduna Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (KADCCIMA), Kaduna. Alh.Shehu Muhammad Shitu is married and blessed with children.

Prof. shehu Usman Abdullahi

Professor Shehu Usman Abdullahi was born on 2nd February, 1951 at Ikara, the headquarters of Ikara Local Government Area of Kaduna State. He attended Ikara Primary School (1958-1960) and Soba Senior Primary School (1961-1963). He attended Provincial Secondary School (now Alhudahuda College), Zaria for his Secondary School education (1964-68) and Government College (now Barewa College), Zaria for his Higher School Certificate studies (1969-70). He obtained a Doctor Of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree from Ahmadu Bello University (ABU), Zaria (1971-76) and a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in the same field from University of Minnesota, USA (1979-82), specializing in Veterinary Urology. He attended the Institute for Policy and Strategic Studies, Kuru-Jos (1990)where he acquired the title of Member of the National Institute (mni). He also holds certificates in University Administration, Computer Appreciation, and Arabic Language. He joined the services of Ahmadu Bello University in 1977 and rose to the rank of Professor in 1989, a period of twelve years. He held the following positions at different times:

  1. Director Veterinary Teaching Hospital, ABU, Zaria (1988-1992);
  2. Deputy Vice-Chancellor, ABU, Zaria (1994-1995)
  3. Academy Provost, Nigerian Defence Academy Kaduna (1996-2004)
    4. and
  4. Vice-Chancellor, ABU, Zaria (2004-2009).
Profesor Shehu Usman Abdullahi served as Chairman/Member of several national and international committees. Currently, he is the Pro-Chancellor and Chairman of the Governing Council of Kaduna State University. Professor Abdullahi has over 100 scientific publications to his credit including in journals (national and international), textbooks, seminars and conferences. He discovered a new treatment for an animal disease called Struvite Urolithiasis (Stone Disease) in 1982. The treatment was approved for use in animals by the American Food and Drug Administration in 1983 and has since then been adopted for human beings. Professor Abdullahi has supervised several postgraduate students who graduated with PhDs and Masters degrees and is still teaching in ABU, Zaria. Professor Abdullahi is a holder of a National Honour, Officer of the Order of the Federal Republic (OFR). He is married and has ten children. Professor Abdullahi has been an ardent golfer since 1998 and was at different times a Member, Board of Trustees of Nigerian Defence Academy Golf Club, Kaduna Golf Club, and Zaria Golf Club. He is also involved in other community services.
