Our Programs

Providing sustainable support for vulnerable children and communities.

In practice

Changing Lives Through Action

Our programs focus on education, healthcare, nutrition, shelter and empowerment to ensure every child has the opportunity to thrive.

Education Support Program

Providing school fees, learning materials, scholarships, mentoring and academic support for vulnerable children.

  • Scholarships
  • School Supplies
  • Academic Mentorship
  • Digital Literacy Training

Healthcare Program

Access to medical care, health awareness, vaccination support and emergency healthcare assistance.

  • Medical Outreach
  • Child Health Monitoring
  • Vaccination Support
  • Health Education

Nutrition Program

Improving children's wellbeing through feeding initiatives and nutrition education.

  • School Feeding
  • Food Relief
  • Nutrition Awareness
  • Emergency Food Support

Shelter & Protection

Providing safe spaces and child protection services for vulnerable children.

  • Emergency Shelter
  • Child Protection Services
  • Safe Housing Initiatives
  • Family Reintegration

Skills Acquisition & Empowerment

Helping young people gain practical skills for self-reliance and economic growth.

  • Vocational Training
  • Entrepreneurship Support
  • Digital Skills Training
  • Career Guidance
