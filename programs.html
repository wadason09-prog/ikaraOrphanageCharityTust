Our Programs

Providing sustainable support for orphans in our communities.

In practice

Changing Lives Through Action

Our programs focus on education, healthcare, nutrition and empowerment to ensure every child has the opportunity to thrive.

Education Support Program

Providing school fees, learning materials, scholarships, mentoring and academic support for registered orphans .

  • Scholarships
  • School Supplies
  • Academic Mentorship
  • Digital Literacy Training

Healthcare Program

Access to medical care, health awareness, vaccination support and emergency healthcare assistance.

  • Medical Outreach
  • Child Health Monitoring
  • Vaccination Support
  • Health Education

Nutrition Program

Improving children's wellbeing through feeding initiatives and nutrition education.

  • Food Distribution
  • Nutrition Awareness
  • Emergency Food Support

Clothing Distribution Program

Providing clothing for registered orphans accross Ikara Local Government.

  • clothes
  • school uniform distribution

Skills Acquisition & Empowerment

Helping young orphans gain practical skills for self-reliance and economic growth.

  • Vocational Training
  • Entrepreneurship Support
  • Digital Skills Training
  • Career Guidance
